About Me

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at Towson University. I received my Ph.D. in 2015 from Dartmouth College, where I studied under the direction of Carl Pomerance. I am especially interested in elementary and combinatorial number theory. You can find information on my research here or on my CV.

I graduated with a B.S. in math and physics from the University of Denver in 2010, and received an A.M. in mathematics from Dartmouth in 2012.
